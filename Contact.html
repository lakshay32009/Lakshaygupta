Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and expertise

What types of cybersecurity projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in threat detection systems, security automation, incident response platforms, and AI-powered security solutions. I work with organizations of all sizes, from startups building their security infrastructure to enterprises optimizing their security operations.

Do you offer remote consulting services?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide through remote consulting. I'm available for video calls, collaborative workshops, and can provide ongoing support through various communication channels. I also travel for on-site engagements when needed.

What's your approach to AI/ML in cybersecurity?

I focus on practical applications of AI/ML that solve real security challenges. This includes anomaly detection, behavioral analysis, automated threat response, and predictive security. I ensure solutions are explainable, ethical, and aligned with business objectives.

How do you stay current with cybersecurity threats and technologies?

I maintain continuous learning through research, conferences, hands-on experimentation, and collaboration with the security community. I regularly contribute to open-source projects and publish research findings to stay at the forefront of the field.

What's your typical engagement process?

I start with an initial consultation to understand your needs and challenges. Then I provide a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and deliverables. Throughout the engagement, I maintain regular communication and provide progress updates. After project completion, I offer ongoing support and knowledge transfer.
